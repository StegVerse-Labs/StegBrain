StegBrain is an AI-assisted operations agent that automates:
- intake normalization
- schema validation
- documentation completeness checks

StegBrain does NOT:
- control equipment
- bypass safety systems
- make operational decisions

---

## Capabilities (v0.1)

### Intake Processing
- Parses HydraSafe and ReactorOps intake forms
- Normalizes into DiamondOps-Core schemas
- Flags missing fields

### Validation
- Runs YieldOS validator on submitted metadata
- Confirms schema compliance
- Generates human-readable summaries

### Reporting
- Generates:
  - intake summary
  - risk flag list
  - next-action checklist

---

## Why This Matters
- Reduces human error
- Speeds onboarding
- Makes DiamondOps scalable without headcount explosions

---

## Positioning
StegBrain is an **assistant**, not an authority.
Final decisions always remain human.
